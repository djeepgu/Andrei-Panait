The begining of 2017 marked my return to the sport I love and have been missing for 16 years. I'm really happy to play for Amsterdam's De Spuyt club.

Short history of Volleybalvereniging De Spuyt

In 1983 a group of enthusiastic volleyball player regulary met in café De Spuyt. They played against each other for a long time and there was no discussion regarding a club, but it started slowly to itch. There was a need for actual competition! This resulted in the creation of De Spuyt volleyball club on 31 August 1987.

Competitions

NeVoBo - Nederlandse Volleybalbond

De Spuyt has 5 women's and 6 men's teams playing various levels in the National Volleyball Championship, they vary from 1st to 6th class.
